# First Grade - Lesson 12: The Weather & Seasons ☀️🌧️❄️  

## 1. Overview  
**Topic:** Learning about different types of weather and the four seasons.  
**Objective:** Identify and describe weather conditions, answer simple weather-related questions, and associate seasons with activities.  

---

## 2. Learning Objectives  
- Students will **recognize and say** different weather conditions.  
- Students will **describe the weather** using simple sentences.  
- Students will answer questions like **"How is the weather today?"**  

---

## 3. Vocabulary & Phrases  

### Weather Conditions 🌤️  
- **Sunny ☀️**  
- **Cloudy ☁️**  
- **Rainy 🌧️**  
- **Windy 🌬️**  
- **Snowy ❄️**  
- **Stormy ⛈️**  

### Seasons & Activities 🍂🏖️  
- **Spring 🌸** – Flowers bloom, birds sing  
- **Summer ☀️** – Hot weather, swimming, ice cream  
- **Autumn 🍂** – Leaves fall, cool weather  
- **Winter ❄️** – Snow, cold, wearing warm clothes  

### Simple Sentences 💬  
- **"How is the weather today?"** → "**It is sunny!**"  
- **"Do you like rainy days?"** → "**Yes, I do! / No, I don’t.**"  
- **"What do you wear in winter?"** → "**I wear a coat.**"  
- **"What do you do in summer?"** → "**I go to the beach!**"  

---

## 4. Pronunciation Tips  
- **Sunny** – /ˈsʌn.i/  
- **Windy** – /ˈwɪn.di/  
- **Snowy** – /ˈsnoʊ.i/  
- **Winter** – /ˈwɪn.tɚ/  

Encourage students to say **each word loudly** and use gestures to represent each weather condition.  

---

## 5. Warm-Up (5–7 Minutes)  

### A) Weather Song 🎶  
_(Tune: "Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star")_  

*How’s the weather, let’s all say,
Is it sunny, is it gray?
Is it raining, is there snow?
Look outside and let us know!*

👉 **Encourage students to look outside and answer: "It is [sunny]!"**  

### B) Weather Flashcards 🌦️  
- Show flashcards with different weather conditions.  
- Ask: **"What is this?"** → Students answer, **"It is cloudy!"**  
- Repeat with **different weather types**.  

---

## 6. Main Activities  

### A) Weather Matching Game (5 Minutes) 🃏  
- Give students **weather flashcards**.  
- Ask them to **match** the weather with **a picture of an activity** (e.g., "Rainy" → Umbrella ☂️).  

### B) Act Like the Weather (10 Minutes) 🌪️  
1. **Teacher says a weather condition**.  
2. Students **act it out**:  
   - **Sunny** → Smile and stretch arms  
   - **Windy** → Move arms like wind  
   - **Rainy** → Pretend to hold an umbrella  
   - **Snowy** → Shiver and hug themselves  

### C) My Favorite Season (10 Minutes) 🎨  
1. **Ask students**: "**What is your favorite season?**"  
2. Students **draw and color a picture** of their favorite season.  
3. They say: **"I like [summer] because I [swim]."**  

---

## 7. Wrap-Up (5 Minutes)  
- **Quick Recap**  
  - Ask: "**What is the weather today?**"  
  - Show a season picture and ask: "**What season is this?**"  

- **Goodbye Weather Song 🎵**  
_(Tune: "If You're Happy and You Know It")_  

*If it’s sunny, clap your hands! ☀️👏
If it’s rainy, stomp your feet! 🌧️👣
If it’s windy, wave your arms! 🌬️👋
If it’s snowy, say "Brrr!" ❄️🥶*

👉 **Students perform each action based on the weather word!**  

---

## 8. Assessment & Reinforcement  

### Quick Check ✅  
- **Point & Say**: Teacher points to a weather picture, and students say the name.  
- **Ask simple questions**: "What do you wear in winter?"  

### Optional Worksheet 📄  
- **Match weather conditions to pictures**.  
- **Draw and label today’s weather**.  

### Homework (If Applicable) 🏠  
- **Look outside tomorrow morning** and tell someone: "**The weather is [sunny]!"**  
- **Draw yourself in your favorite season and write the name**.  

---

## 9. Tips for the Teacher ✨  
- **Use Real Weather**: Ask about the weather **every morning**.  
- **Encourage Speaking**: Every student should **say a weather word aloud**.  
- **Make It Fun**: **Songs, games, and role-play** keep students engaged.  
- **Reinforce Daily**: Ask about the weather **before going home**.  

---

**End of First Grade - Lesson 12: The Weather & Seasons ☀️🌧️❄️**  

Feel free to adapt based on your classroom needs! 😊
